How the system decides

Two paths to a signal

Not every opinion becomes a trade. The system has two clear approval paths:

🏆 Full Consensus 3 or more agents independently see the same opportunity — the signal fires directly.
⚡ Verified Entry Only 2 agents agree? The system doesn't guess. It calls the Macro Agent — which checks real-time dollar strength, bond yields, and Fed tone. If the macro picture confirms, the signal passes. If not, it stays silent.

Questions before joining

Frequently asked questions

Is this fully automated?

Yes as a signal-following system: market scan, filtering, Telegram alerts, open-trade follow-up, and reports are automated.

Does it place trades in my account?

No. It provides signals, trade updates, and performance tracking. You remain responsible for execution and risk.

How often does monitoring run?

New opportunity analysis runs every 5 minutes, and open trade updates also run every 5 minutes.

Does it guarantee profit?

No. Trading involves risk. SmartSignal Pro provides organization, signals, and tracking, but results are not guaranteed.

Does it use AI?

Yes, as an extra review layer for signals, news, reports, and learning — not as a replacement for risk control.

What is RR Capture?

It compares what a trade actually captured with the planned reward, helping show exit and management quality.

Do reports show more than profit/loss?

Yes. Reports can include RR quality, best sessions, news impact, market fit, and practical next actions.
